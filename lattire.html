L'attire

Boldness, creativity, uniqueness and elegance. L'attire is an event where you need to present all these skills to put yourself ahead of others. Follow the trend or set a new one. All that matters is the outcome. So, select your front man, create a work of art and prove yourself on the dais. Step up and conquer L'attire.

Rules

One team representing each school comprising 3 models and 3 designers.
